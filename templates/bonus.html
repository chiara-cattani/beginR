Tips & Tricks
  • Run code: Ctrl+Enter (line) / Ctrl+Shift+Enter (script)
  • Pipe smart: Use %>% or |> for clear, readable chains
  • Name clean: lowercase + _ (e.g., sdtm_data)
  • Check data: summary(), unique(), count()
  • Find NAs: is.na() or count(is.na())
